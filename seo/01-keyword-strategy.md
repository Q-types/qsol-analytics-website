## Local SEO Strategy

### Approach
QSol Analytics serves UK SMEs primarily online/remotely. Local SEO focuses on:
1. UK geographic signals without creating fake local offices
2. Google Business Profile (if home office qualifies)
3. Industry-specific UK terminology

### UK Geographic Signals
- Use "UK" in key page titles and H1s
- Reference UK-specific context: HMRC, VAT, GDPR-UK, Companies House
- Use British English spelling throughout
- Mention serving "businesses across the UK"
- Include case studies with UK-based (anonymised) examples

### Google Business Profile
**If registering (home office qualifies):**
- Category: "Business Consultant" or "Data Analytics Service"
- Service area: United Kingdom
- Photos: Professional headshot, work examples (anonymised dashboards)
- Posts: Monthly insights, offers
- Q&A: Seed with common questions

**If not registering:**
- Focus on organic SEO and content authority
- Build citations on UK business directories (Yell, FreeIndex)
- LinkedIn company page with UK location

### Industry-Specific UK Terms
Include naturally in content:
- "SME" (not "SMB")
- "Ltd" / "Limited company"
- "VAT returns" / "Making Tax Digital"
- "GDPR" (UK retained version)
- "Month-end reporting" / "Board pack"
- "FD" / "Finance Director" (not "CFO" primarily)

## On-Page SEO Specifications

### Title Tag Formula
```
[Primary Keyword] | [Benefit/Qualifier] | QSol Analytics
```

**Examples:**
- Home: "Data Science & Automation for UK SMEs | QSol Analytics"
- Automation: "Business Automation Services | Results in Weeks | QSol Analytics"
- Blog: "[Article Title] | QSol Analytics Insights"

**Constraints:**
- Max 60 characters
- Primary keyword near start
- Brand at end

### Meta Description Formula
```
[Problem/Hook]. [Solution]. [Proof/CTA]. [60-155 chars]
```

**Examples:**
- Home: "Drowning in spreadsheets? QSol Analytics delivers automation, dashboards, and forecasting for UK SMEs—measurable results in weeks, not months. Book a discovery call."
- Automation: "Manual reporting eating your week? Our automation services give operations teams their time back. From prototype to production in 2-4 weeks."

**Constraints:**
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- End with CTA or value statement

### H1/H2 Structure Template

**Service Pages:**
```
H1: [Service] for UK SMEs
H2: The Problem: [Pain point]
H2: Our Approach: [Solution method]
H2: What You Get: [Deliverables]
H2: How It Works: [Process steps]
H2: Results You Can Expect
H2: Ready to [CTA verb]?
```

**Blog Articles:**
```
H1: [Keyword-Rich Title]
H2: [Section 1 - often "Why this matters"]
H3: [Subsection if needed]
H2: [Section 2 - the core content]
H2: [Section 3 - practical application]
H2: [Section 4 - next steps/summary]
```

## Technical SEO Checklist

### Crawlability
- [ ] XML sitemap at /sitemap.xml
- [ ] Sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
- [ ] robots.txt allows all important pages
- [ ] No orphan pages (all linked from navigation or content)
- [ ] Max 3 clicks from homepage to any page

### Indexability
- [ ] Canonical tags on all pages (self-referencing)
- [ ] No duplicate content issues
- [ ] No thin pages (<300 words) unless appropriate (contact, legal)
- [ ] Pagination handled correctly (if applicable)

### Performance
- [ ] Core Web Vitals passing
- [ ] Images optimised (WebP, lazy loading)
- [ ] CSS/JS minified
- [ ] Fonts preloaded

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Link Hierarchy
1. **Homepage** links to: Services overview, Case studies, About, top blog posts
2. **Services overview** links to: All 4 service detail pages
3. **Service pages** link to: Related case study, 2-3 relevant blog posts, contact
4. **Blog posts** link to: Relevant service page, 2-3 related posts, lead magnet
5. **Case studies** link to: Relevant service, contact

### Anchor Text Guidelines
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Include keywords naturally (not forced)
- Vary anchor text for same destination
- Internal links: 3-5 per page minimum

### Cross-Linking Rules
- Every service page links to at least one case study
- Every blog post links to parent service page
- Every page links to contact or CTA
- Footer links to all main pages
