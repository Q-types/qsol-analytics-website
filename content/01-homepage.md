## Process Section

### H2
How It Works

### H3
A lightweight process designed for busy teams

**Step 1: Discovery (30 min call)**
We discuss your current setup, pain points, and goals. No jargon, no hard sell. You'll leave with a clear sense of whether we're a fit.

**Step 2: Assessment (1-3 days)**
We review your existing data, systems, and workflows. You receive a written assessment highlighting quick wins and strategic opportunities.

**Step 3: Build (2-4 weeks)**
We build a working solution—an automated report, a dashboard, a forecast model—that you can test with real data. You see results before the main investment.

**Step 4: Refine (1 week)**
We refine based on feedback and ensure everything works in your environment with your real data.

**Step 5: Deploy & Support**
We document everything and hand over—or provide ongoing support. You choose the level of involvement.
