## General Questions

### H2
General Questions

**Q: What exactly does QSol Analytics do?**

We help UK SMEs use their data better—through automation (making manual processes automatic), dashboards (turning data into visible insights), segmentation (understanding your customers), and forecasting (predicting what's coming).

Think of us as a practical data science partner for businesses that are too big for spreadsheets but not big enough for an in-house data team.

---

**Q: Who do you work with?**

We primarily work with UK SMEs—businesses with 10 to 500 employees, typically in operations-heavy industries: logistics, manufacturing, professional services, property and facilities, education providers, and ecommerce.

Our typical contact is an Operations Director, Finance Director, or Managing Director who knows data could help but doesn't have the internal expertise to make it happen.

---

**Q: How is this different from hiring a consultant from a big firm?**

Four main differences:

1. **You work with me directly.** No juniors doing the actual work while seniors move on to the next pitch.
2. **We're faster.** Working prototypes in 2-4 weeks instead of 3-month scoping phases.
3. **Signal over noise.** We focus on what matters—not 100-page reports you'll never read.
4. **We're more affordable.** No expensive offices and partner structures to support. Savings passed to you.

---

**Q: How is this different from buying off-the-shelf software?**

Software products are built for general use cases. They're great when they fit your needs—and frustrating when they don't.

We build solutions around your specific workflows, data sources, and requirements. More work upfront, but the result fits like a glove instead of like a compromise.

For some needs, off-the-shelf is the right answer. We'll tell you if that's the case.

## Process & Timeline

### H2
Process & Timeline {#process}

**Q: How does a typical project start?**

With a 30-minute discovery call. We discuss what you're trying to achieve, what data you have, and what's getting in the way. No commitment—just a conversation to see if there's a fit.

If there is, we'll propose a specific scope, timeline, and fixed price. You review, we refine, and then we start.

---

**Q: How long do projects take?**

It varies by scope, but typical timelines:

- Simple automation: 1-2 weeks
- Dashboard build: 2-4 weeks
- Customer segmentation analysis: 2-4 weeks
- Forecasting system: 4-8 weeks
- Comprehensive multi-workstream project: 2-3 months

We work in phases—prototype first, refine second—so you see results early.

---

**Q: How much involvement is required from my team?**

Less than you'd expect. We need:

- Access to relevant data (we'll help you identify what's needed)
- A point of contact for questions and feedback
- An hour or two per week for check-ins and reviews

We're designed to fit around busy teams, not consume them.

## Pricing & Investment

### H2
Pricing & Investment {#pricing}

**Q: How much does this cost?**

Projects typically range from £3,000 for focused automations to £25,000+ for comprehensive analytics implementations.

We provide fixed quotes after the discovery call—based on specific scope, not day rates. No surprise invoices.

Example ranges:
- Report automation: £2,000-£4,000
- Department dashboard: £3,000-£6,000
- Customer segmentation: £2,500-£18,000
- Forecasting system: £6,000-£15,000

---

**Q: Do you offer ongoing support?**

Yes. After initial projects, many clients move to a retained arrangement—monthly hours for ad-hoc questions, improvements, and new analysis.

**Retained support typically ranges from £500-2,000/month** depending on scope—covering ad-hoc questions, dashboard updates, proactive monitoring, and priority access for urgent requests.

We also offer "check-ins" for completed systems: periodic reviews to ensure everything is still working as expected.

---

**Q: What's the payment structure?**

Typically:
- 30% upfront on project start
- 40% at prototype delivery
- 30% on final delivery

For retained arrangements: monthly invoicing.

We're flexible for the right projects—let's discuss what works for you.

---

**Q: What if I'm not sure if this is worth the investment?**

Start with an Assessment (£500-1,500, 1-3 days). You'll receive:

- Written review of your current data landscape
- Specific opportunities identified with estimated ROI
- Recommended project scope and approach
- Honest assessment of feasibility and risks

If we don't find opportunities worth pursuing, you've capped your downside at the assessment fee. If we do find value, the assessment fee applies toward the project.

No pressure, no wasted budget. Many Finance Directors prefer this approach—it's due diligence with a clear deliverable.

## Data & Technical Questions

### H2
Data & Technical {#technical}

**Q: What if our data is messy?**

Most data is messy. It's not a blocker—it's often the highest-ROI work we do.

Part of most engagements is cleaning, standardising, and documenting data. We'll be honest about what's possible given your data quality, and we'll help improve it.

---

**Q: Do we need to buy any software?**

Usually not. We work with tools you likely already have (Excel, SQL databases, Python) and open-source options (Metabase, Superset, Streamlit) when needed.

If a paid tool genuinely adds value (e.g., Power BI), we'll recommend it—but we're not resellers pushing licences.

---

**Q: Will we own the code/models you build?**

Yes. Everything we build is yours—code, models, documentation. No proprietary black boxes you can't access.

---

**Q: What happens if our requirements change mid-project?**

Small changes are part of any project—we expect and absorb them.

Larger scope changes are handled transparently: we'll outline the impact, propose adjusted timeline/price, and let you decide how to proceed.

No nasty surprises.

## Automation Questions {#automation}

### H2
Automation Questions

**Q: What kinds of processes can be automated?**

Almost any repetitive data task:
- Pulling data from systems and creating formatted reports
- Copying data between systems
- Sending scheduled emails with updated information
- Validating data and flagging errors
- Any "we do this the same way every week" process

If a human is doing it repeatedly and a computer could do it reliably, it's a candidate.

---

**Q: How do we maintain the automation after you leave?**

We document everything: what the automation does, how to run it, how to modify it. We also offer training sessions and ongoing support options.

The goal is for you to be self-sufficient—but we're here if you need us.

## Security & Data Handling {#security}

### H2
Security & Data Handling

**Q: How do you handle our data securely?**

We take data security seriously. Our approach:

- **Access:** We only access data needed for the specific project, using credentials you control
- **Storage:** Project data stays on your systems where possible. When we need to work with extracts, we use encrypted storage and delete copies after project completion
- **Transfer:** Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). We don't send data via email attachments
- **People:** Only the project team (typically just David) accesses your data. No offshore teams, no subcontractors without your explicit approval
- **Documentation:** We document what data we accessed and when

---

**Q: Are you GDPR compliant?**

Yes. We operate as a data processor when handling your data, and we're happy to sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) if your compliance team requires one.

We don't collect personal data beyond what's needed for the project. We don't sell or share client data. We delete project data after completion unless you ask us to retain it.

For projects involving customer data, we can advise on GDPR considerations—but we recommend involving your legal/compliance team for anything sensitive.

---

**Q: What about audit requirements?**

We build with auditability in mind:

- Clear documentation of data sources, transformations, and logic
- Version-controlled code so you can see what changed and when
- Outputs that can be traced back to source data
- Plain-English explanations of how models work

If you need outputs that satisfy external auditors (bank, investor, regulator), tell us upfront. We'll design accordingly.

---

**Q: What do we actually receive at project end?**

Every project includes a handover pack:

- **Working solution:** Tested, documented, deployed in your environment
- **Source code:** Version-controlled (Git), fully commented, yours to keep
- **Documentation:** Setup guide, user guide, technical reference
- **Data dictionary:** What each field means, where it comes from
- **Assumptions log:** Every decision and why it was made

You own everything. No black boxes, no vendor lock-in, no "call us to make changes."

---

**Q: Will we need you after the project ends?**

You shouldn't *need* us—that's the point. Everything is documented for self-sufficiency.

That said, many clients choose retained support (£500-2,000/month) for:
- Ad-hoc questions and troubleshooting
- Dashboard updates and new metrics
- Model retraining and accuracy monitoring
- Priority access for urgent requests

Optional, not required. We'll tell you honestly whether it's worth it for your situation.

## Finance Director Questions {#finance}

### H2
Finance Director Questions

**Q: Can you help with board pack preparation?**

Yes. Common scenarios:

- **Automated data gathering:** Pull numbers from multiple systems into a single source
- **Dashboard for board metrics:** Real-time view of KPIs so the pack is always current
- **Variance analysis:** Automated comparison to budget/forecast with commentary prompts
- **Cash flow forecasting:** Rolling predictions based on historical patterns

We can't do the narrative for you—but we can make sure the numbers are ready, accurate, and defensible.

---

**Q: How do you handle VAT/HMRC requirements?**

We're not accountants, but we build systems that make compliance easier:

- Clean data exports in formats your accountant can use
- Audit trails showing data lineage
- Automated checks for common data quality issues

If you have specific HMRC reporting requirements, we'll work with your finance team to ensure outputs meet their needs.

---

**Q: Are your numbers defensible for external stakeholders?**

Yes, with caveats:

- We document methodology clearly so you can explain how numbers were derived
- We validate accuracy against known baselines before deployment
- We provide confidence ranges, not false precision
- We're transparent about limitations and assumptions

If numbers need to satisfy banks, investors, or regulators, we'll design with that standard in mind. Tell us the audience upfront.

---

## Forecasting Questions {#forecasting}

### H2
Forecasting Questions

**Q: How accurate are the forecasts?**

Accuracy depends on data quality, predictability of what you're forecasting, and the forecast horizon.

We measure and report accuracy transparently. You'll know the error ranges and confidence levels—not just point estimates.

A forecast that's 80% accurate is often dramatically better than gut feel or spreadsheet extrapolation.

---

**Q: What data do we need for forecasting?**

Ideally: 2+ years of historical data for the metric you want to forecast, at the time granularity you need (daily, weekly, monthly).

We can work with less, but accuracy improves with more history. We'll assess your data and be honest about what's achievable.
