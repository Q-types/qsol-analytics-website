## What You Get Section

### H2
Typical Segmentation Deliverables

**RFM Segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)**
Classic approach that works. Customers scored and grouped by how recently they bought, how often, and how much they spend.
*Output: Customer list with segment labels + tailored strategy per segment.*

**Churn Risk Scoring**
Predictive model identifying which customers are most likely to leave in the next 30/60/90 days.
*Output: Risk scores integrated into CRM + recommended retention actions.*

**Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**
Projected value of each customer over their relationship with you. Essential for acquisition spend decisions.
*Output: CLV estimates per customer + segment-level averages.*

**Behavioural Clustering**
Machine learning to identify natural groups in your customer base based on behaviour patterns.
*Output: Cluster definitions + customer assignments + insight report.*

**Personalisation Recommendations**
Specific, actionable suggestions for how to treat different segments differently—in marketing, pricing, or service.
*Output: Strategy document + implementation roadmap.*
