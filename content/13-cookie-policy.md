### H1
Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2024

This policy explains how QSol Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

---

### H2
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, including remembering preferences, analysing traffic, and enabling certain functionality.

---

### H2
Our Approach to Cookies

**We take a privacy-first approach.**

Our website uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics service that:
- Does not use cookies
- Does not track individuals
- Does not collect personal data
- Is GDPR compliant by design

This means we can understand how people use our website in aggregate (total visits, popular pages) without tracking you individually.

---

### H2
Cookies We Use

**Currently, this website does not set any cookies.**

Because we use Plausible Analytics (cookieless), no tracking cookies are placed on your device.

If this changes in future (for example, if we add live chat or remember user preferences), we will update this policy and implement appropriate consent mechanisms.

---

### H2
Third-Party Cookies

**Contact form:**
Our contact form is handled by Formspree. Formspree may set functional cookies necessary to submit the form securely. These are essential cookies that do not track you for marketing purposes.

**External links:**
If you click links to third-party sites (e.g., LinkedIn, Calendly), those sites may set their own cookies. Please review their cookie policies.

**Embedded content:**
If we embed content from other sites (e.g., YouTube videos in blog posts), those embeds may set cookies. We aim to use privacy-respecting embed options where available.

---

### H2
Your Choices

Since we don't use tracking cookies, no action is required from you.

If you want to manage cookies from other websites, you can:

**In your browser:**
- Block or delete cookies via browser settings
- Use private/incognito browsing mode
- Install privacy-focused browser extensions

**Browser guides:**
- Chrome: support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
- Firefox: support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies
- Safari: support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies
- Edge: support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/cookies

---

### H2
Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature. Since we don't track individuals anyway, this setting has no additional effect on our website—you're already not being tracked.
