## Approach Section

### H2
Our Approach

### The QSol Method

**AI That Serves the Business**
We're fluent in machine learning, statistical modelling, and data engineering—but we never apply these techniques just because we can. Every tool earns its place by delivering measurable results. When a simple solution works better than a complex one, we'll tell you. When AI genuinely adds value, we'll explain exactly why and how.

**1. Signal Over Noise**
Most data analysis produces more confusion, not less. We focus obsessively on surfacing what matters—the metrics that drive decisions, the patterns that predict outcomes, the anomalies that demand attention.

Physics training helps here. Finding signal in noise is what physicists do.

**2. Rapid Prototypes**
Ideas are cheap; working systems are valuable. We build prototypes early—in weeks, not months—so you can see and test before committing to full implementation.

If a prototype doesn't work, we've learned something important at low cost.

**3. Measurable Outcomes**
Every project starts with a clear definition of success. What will change? How will we measure it? This keeps us honest and keeps the work focused.

No vanity metrics. No dashboards that look impressive but don't drive action.

**4. Practical Engineering**
Code should be readable, testable, and maintainable. Documentation should exist. Handovers should be clean. We build systems you can live with long-term—not clever hacks that only we understand.

We build primarily with Python—the leading language for data science—giving you flexibility, transparency, and no vendor lock-in. Our tech choices are deliberate: proven tools, clean code, clear documentation.

**5. Plain English**
If we can't explain it clearly, we don't understand it well enough. Jargon is often a sign of unclear thinking. We explain what we're doing and why—in terms that make sense to business people.

## Who We're Not For

### H2
Who We're Not For

We're not the right fit for everyone. To save us both time:

**We're probably not for you if:**
- You need a full-time data team replacement (we're project-based or retained advisory)
- You want AI/ML applied regardless of whether simpler solutions work better
- You need someone to "just make the dashboard look nice" without addressing underlying data issues
- Your timeline is "yesterday" with no flexibility
- You want to outsource thinking, not collaborate on solutions

**We're a great fit if:**
- You have real business problems that data could solve—but lack the expertise to make it happen
- You value outcomes over activity, simplicity over complexity
- You want to understand what we build, not just receive a black box
- You're willing to invest time in the discovery process to get the scope right
- You want a partner who'll tell you when a project isn't worth doing
