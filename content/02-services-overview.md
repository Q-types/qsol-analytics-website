### Service 1: Automation & Workflow

**The Problem:**
Your team spends hours every week on tasks a machine should handle. Copying data between systems. Reformatting reports. Chasing the same information across five spreadsheets.

**Typical Deliverables:**
- Automated report generation and distribution
- Data pipeline from source systems to dashboards

- API integrations between platforms
