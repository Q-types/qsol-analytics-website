## What You Get Section

### H2
Typical Automation Deliverables

**Automated Reporting**
Reports generated and distributed automatically—daily, weekly, monthly. No more "I'll get to it when I have time."
*Example: Daily sales report pulled from three systems, formatted in Excel, emailed to regional managers by 8am.*

**Data Pipelines**
Scheduled extraction, transformation, and loading of data from source systems to dashboards or warehouses.
*Example: CRM data combined with ERP data, cleaned, and loaded into a reporting database every night.*

**System Integrations**
Connect systems that don't natively talk to each other. Push and pull data via APIs or file transfers.
*Example: Orders from your ecommerce platform automatically pushed to your fulfilment system.*

**Data Quality Checks**
Automated validation that catches problems before they reach downstream reports.
*Example: Alert when customer records are missing postcodes or when sales figures look anomalous.*

**Scheduled Tasks**
Any repeatable data task you do manually today can likely be automated and scheduled.
*Example: Weekly export of aged debtors, formatted for credit control team review.*
