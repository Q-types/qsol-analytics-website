## Problem Section

### H2
The Problem: Data Everywhere, Insight Nowhere

You have data. Lots of it. What you don't have:

- **A single version of the truth.** Sales says one number. Finance says another. Operations has a third.
- **Timely insights.** By the time you see a trend, it's old news.
- **Self-service answers.** Every question requires someone to build a new report.
- **Actionable KPIs.** Dashboards show lots of numbers—but which ones actually drive decisions?

You've probably tried off-the-shelf BI tools. They're powerful, but configuring them to match your business takes months—and often never quite works.

## Solution Section

### H2
Our Approach: Dashboards Built for Your Business

We don't sell software. We build dashboards around how your business actually works—the metrics that matter to your specific operations, in formats your team will actually use.

**What Makes Our Dashboards Different:**

- **Decision-focused:** Every metric earns its place. No dashboard bloat.
- **Designed for your users:** Executives get summaries; managers get drill-down; analysts get data access.
- **Trustworthy:** Clear data sources, update timestamps, and data quality indicators.
- **Maintainable:** Built so you can modify and extend—not locked into our ongoing involvement.

**Technology Options:**
We're tool-agnostic. We'll recommend and implement what fits:
- Power BI (common in Microsoft environments)
- Metabase / Superset (open-source, self-hosted)
- Custom dashboards (Python + Streamlit for specific needs)
- Google Looker Studio (if you're in Google Workspace)

## What You Get Section

### H2
Typical Dashboard Deliverables

**Executive Dashboard**
High-level KPIs for leadership. Revenue, margin, operational health, key trends. Updated automatically, always current.
*Example: Weekly performance summary with drill-down to regional/product detail.*

**Operational Dashboards**
Real-time or near-real-time metrics for operational managers. What needs attention today.
*Example: Live order pipeline, delivery SLAs, inventory alerts.*

**Financial Reporting**
Month-end pack components automated. Variance analysis, trend charts, forecast vs. actual.
*Example: P&L dashboard refreshed daily, board pack generated monthly.*

**Sales & Marketing Analytics**
Pipeline health, conversion rates, campaign performance, customer acquisition cost.
*Example: CRM data visualised with funnel analysis and segment comparisons.*

**Custom KPI Tracking**
Whatever metrics matter to your business, surfaced clearly and tracked over time.
*Example: Fleet utilisation, call centre metrics, student enrolment pipeline.*

## Process Section

### H2
How We Build Dashboards

**Step 1: Discovery (30 min call)**
We discuss what questions you need answered, who needs to see what, and what data exists today.

**Step 2: Assessment (1-3 days)**
We review your data sources: quality, accessibility, and what's needed to make them dashboard-ready.

**Step 3: Build (2-4 weeks)**
We design wireframes, connect data, build visualisations, and refine based on your feedback.

**Step 4: Refine (1 week)**
Final adjustments based on user testing and feedback. Ensuring everything works for all users.

**Step 5: Deploy & Support**
We deploy, document, and train your team. You're self-sufficient, or we stay involved—your choice.
