## Problem Section

### H2
The Problem: Always Reacting, Never Anticipating

In operations-heavy businesses, surprises are expensive:

- **Demand spikes** catch you off guard—stockouts, overtime, unhappy customers
- **Demand drops** leave you overstaffed and overstocked
- **Anomalies** hide in the data until they become incidents
- **Cash flow** is a guessing game—you plan for average but live with variance
- **Planning cycles** rely on spreadsheet extrapolation that nobody trusts

You know the patterns exist in your data. You just can't see them—until it's too late.

### H2
Our Approach: Time-Series Intelligence

We apply proven forecasting methods and anomaly detection techniques to your operational data—so you can anticipate instead of react.

**Forecasting — How We Predict What's Coming:**
- **Time-tested statistical methods** for predictable patterns (like seasonal sales or weekly order volumes)
- **Advanced machine learning** when your data has complex relationships that simpler methods miss
- **Combined approaches** that blend multiple methods to improve accuracy
- **Ranges, not just single numbers** so you can plan for best-case, worst-case, and most-likely scenarios

*What this means for you:* We use proven techniques that have worked for decades, enhanced with modern methods where they genuinely help. You get predictions you can act on, not just impressive-sounding jargon.

**Anomaly Detection — Catching Problems Early:**
- **Automated monitoring** that watches your key metrics 24/7
- **Pattern recognition** across multiple data streams simultaneously
- **Instant alerts** when something looks unusual—before it becomes a crisis
- **Investigation shortcuts** pointing to likely causes, not just "something's wrong"

*What this means for you:* Your team gets notified when metrics deviate from normal—with enough context to investigate quickly. No more discovering problems from customer complaints.

**What Makes This Practical:**
